KALI PUJA




Here are 10 important points about Kali Puja:
  1. Kali Puja worships Goddess Kali, symbolizing destruction of evil, time, power, and motherly protection.
  2. It is celebrated on the new moon night, especially in West Bengal and eastern India.
  3. Homes and pandals are decorated with lights, diyas, and colorful designs.
  4. Devotees offer flowers, sweets, and prayers to seek strength and protection.
  5. The festival coincides with Diwali, spreading devotion, light, and spiritual energy.
