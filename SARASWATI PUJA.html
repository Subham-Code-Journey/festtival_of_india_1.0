SARASWATI PUJA




Here are 10 important points about Saraswati Puja:
  1. Saraswati Puja worships Goddess Saraswati, the deity of knowledge, wisdom, music, and learning.
  2. It is mainly celebrated by students, teachers, and artists with great devotion.
  3. Books, musical instruments, and pens are placed near the goddess for blessings.
  4. People wear yellow clothes, symbolizing energy, learning, and prosperity.
    5. The festival inspires learning, creativity, discipline, and respect for education.
