JAGADHATRI PUJA




Here are 5 important points about Jagadhatri Puja:
  1. Jagadhatri Puja celebrates Goddess Jagadhatri, a form of Durga, symbolizing power, compassion, and protection.
  2. It is mainly celebrated in West Bengal, especially in Chandannagar and Krishnanagar.
  3. Beautifully decorated idols and grand processions are special attractions of Jagadhatri Puja.
  4. Devotees offer prayers, flowers, and sweets, seeking peace, strength, and prosperity.
  5. The festival ends with immersion of idols, spreading devotion, harmony, and happiness.
